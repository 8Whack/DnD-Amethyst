Welcome to DnD Amethyst!

DnD Amethyst is an online tool for new players looking to get into Dungeons and Dragons: 5th Edition.
On this site you'll find some helpful tools to run a successful DnD Campaign. Everything here is material that Wizards of the Coast has made available to the public.
Use the menu on the left to navigate, and have fun!
